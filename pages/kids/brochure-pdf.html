Proprietary Frameworks They'll Learn

SPARK
How to Talk to AI
Specificity - Be precise about what you want
Persona - Tell AI who it should be
Action - Define the exact task
Result - Describe the desired output
Keep refining - Iterate until perfect
THINK
How to Use AI Responsibly
True? - Is the information accurate?
Helpful? - Does this add value?
Inspiring? - Is it positive and constructive?
Necessary? - Is AI the right tool here?
Kind? - Does it respect others?
