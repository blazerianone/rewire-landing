Proprietary Frameworks

Two Frameworks They'll Master for Life

These aren't just course concepts — they're mental models for working with AI effectively and responsibly.

SPARK

How to Talk to AI

A 5-step framework for writing prompts that get exactly what you want from AI tools.

  • S
    Specificity Be precise about what you want
  • P
    Persona Tell AI who it should be
  • A
    Action Define the exact task
  • R
    Result Describe the desired output
  • K
    Keep refining Iterate until perfect
THINK

How to Use AI Responsibly

A 5-step framework for making ethical decisions when working with AI tools.

  • T
    True? Is the information accurate?
  • H
    Helpful? Does this add value?
  • I
    Inspiring? Is it positive and constructive?
  • N
    Necessary? Is AI the right tool here?
  • K
    Kind? Does it respect others?

Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is this for?
This course is designed for students aged 10-16. The curriculum is flexible enough to challenge older students while remaining accessible for younger ones. The instructor adapts the pace based on the batch composition.
Does my child need coding experience?
No coding experience is required. We use AI-assisted tools that allow students to build websites and apps without writing traditional code. The focus is on understanding concepts and creating real projects, not memorizing syntax.
What device does my child need?
A laptop or desktop computer with a stable internet connection. Tablets and phones are not suitable for the hands-on building activities. Chrome or Firefox browser is recommended.
What if my child misses a session?
All sessions are recorded and shared within 24 hours so students can catch up quickly. If your child misses multiple sessions, our team will guide them on how to get back on track.
What if my child is shy or introverted?
Our small batch size (max 30) ensures every student gets attention. The instructor creates a supportive environment where students can participate at their comfort level. Camera is optional during regular sessions, but encouraged for Demo Day.
Why is the fee ₹14,999?
This is a live, guided cohort — not a video course. Your child gets 21 live sessions, project feedback, small-batch attention (max 30), parent updates, session recordings, Demo Day, and a full refund option after the first session if it doesn't feel right.
How does the refund guarantee work?
After the first session, if you or your child feel this isn't the right fit, simply email us within 24 hours and we'll refund 100% of your fee. No questions asked, no hassle.
Will my child's projects stay live after the course?
Yes. The website and web app they build will remain live on the internet. They'll have full ownership of their projects and can continue updating them after the course ends.
