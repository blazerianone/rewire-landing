The AI PM Gap Is Costing You Roles

You've got the PM fundamentals. But AI PM interviews expose gaps you can't fake — and interviewers know it in the first 10 minutes.

"Walk me through an AI product you'd build"

You scramble for an answer. They wanted a structured framework — opportunity sizing, technical feasibility, success metrics. You gave them vague ideas.

"How would you spec this AI feature?"

You write PRDs all day. But AI specs need accuracy targets, failure modes, edge cases, latency constraints. Different playbook. You don't have it.

"Can you explain RAG vs fine-tuning?"

ML engineers test you. Can you hold your own or do you nod and hope? They're deciding if you're a PM they want to work with — or work around.

"Show me AI work you've done"

The interview ends here. No portfolio, no proof. They'll pick the candidate who can link a PRD, show a teardown, and defend their AI thinking.
